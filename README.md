## Paper

L2DiffM: LLM-Guided Variational Expert Diffusion for Target-Aware 3D Molecular Generation
![image](https://github.com/ReLHHH/L2DiffM/blob/main/fig1.png)
**Authors**: Jiahao Liu et al.

### Benchmark Results

Evaluated on PDBbind and CrossDocked benchmarks, L2DiffM achieves consistent improvements in molecular stability, binding affinity, drug-likeness, and conformational rationality.
